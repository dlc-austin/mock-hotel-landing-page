Here you'll find some frequently asked questions about our hotel. We hope that you find it interesting and informative. These are the questions that people call our front desk with most often.

What inspired "The Sakura Hotel" theme?

I appreciate Japan's culture a lot. I never been, but it's on my bucket list to explore. I love everything about Japan, from the landscape, to anime, and finally food. Cherry blossoms are such beautiful trees with pink vibrant colors. I think it would be a luxury if a hotel would be built with cherry blossoms surrounding it.

What was your thought process behind the color palette for the website?

I decided to choose link pink, yellow, and green to symbolize the appearance of the cherry blossom. I also decided to choose blue to symbolize the clear blue sky in Japan.

What kind of events would you provide at the hotel?

I'm a big fan of Japanese culture. These are some ideas that I have in mind:

  • Learning Basic Japanese Words
  • Learning how to make sushi
  • Learning how to make ramen/noodles

What kind of amenities would you provide at the hotel?

I want to put an outdoor rock climbing wall and golf course. Physical activities while being surrounded by a scenery environment with family and friends sound like a great time.
